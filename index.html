Notes

Course work as an MSc student

How long is the MSc program at the University of Alberta?

First 5 semesters are guaranteed funding. If you need more, you may get TA or RA depending on supervisor and department.

How many courses do you need to do?

Thesis-based MSc students must take five courses. One mandatory: Research and Teaching Methodology. Leaves four electives.

Do you need to finish courses by a specific time?

Advised to finish within the first year (Fall + Winter). Then summer onwards is research only.

What if I don’t finish in the first year?

You can spread them over up to 4 semesters while also doing research.

How much time is spent on courses vs. research?

Research starts once courses are done. Then nearly all time is on research.

TA work

How many hours do you need to work as a TA?

12 hours per week.

Do you need to do more than 12 hours?

No, but it depends on course prep difficulty. For unfamiliar courses, prep may take longer.

How to avoid this?

Before each term, you fill out a skills form for TA assignment. Be honest. Not selecting many skills will not harm funding.
